AIRLANGGA ESPORTS

Airlangga Esport is a dynamic community founded to empower young talents in the esports field, primarily within Universitas Airlangga and the surrounding regions. Driven by a mission to foster teamwork, sportsmanship, and achievement, Airlangga Esport enhances the role of students and youth in competitive gaming, both professionally and recreationally.​

More than just a gaming club, Airlangga Esport instills strong family values and collaboration among its members. The organization believes that esports is not only about winning, but also character building, developing soft skills, and nurturing independence. Every member is encouraged to be proactive, creative, and adaptive in the face of challenges inside and outside the game arena. Hence, various training sessions, internal tournaments, and participation in external competitions are regularly held so that individual talents and interests keep growing and thriving.​

The esports industry offers vast opportunities for students—from non-academic accomplishments to establishing professional networks within the digital world. Airlangga Esport prepares young generations to compete at national and international levels while fostering social responsibility and positive contributions to the broader community. The synergy of passion, discipline, and innovation is the foundation for Airlangga Esport as a platform for creativity and achievement in the modern era.​

Joining Airlangga Esport means becoming part of a journey to build community, friendship, and the future of Indonesia’s gaming scene—a future full of possibilities. Together, we do more than play—we shape the change.
